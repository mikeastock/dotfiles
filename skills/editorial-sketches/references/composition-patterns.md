# Composition Patterns And Originality Rules

## Basic Structure Types

Choose one structure type. Do not combine too many.

### Workflow

Good for: input -> processing -> output, content production, AI workflows, automation chains.

How to draw it: input on the left, Xiaohei or a strange machine processing in the middle, output on the right, with orange arrows showing the primary flow.

### System Slice

Good for: information sources, filters, databases, renderers, or part of an agent system.

How to draw it: show only 3-5 core modules. Xiaohei participates in one key action.

### Before/After Contrast

Good for: messy/ordered, manual/automated, scattered/gathered, anxious/stable.

How to draw it: messy state on the left, stable state on the right, orange arrow in the middle. The character can be more exaggerated.

### Role State

Good for: user pain, creator state, too many tools, information anxiety, stuck-to-running progress.

How to draw it: 2-4 small states, each with one short label.

### Conceptual Metaphor

Good for: content factory, information warehouse, mental black box, workflow machine, automated daily report.

How to draw it: one large strange object or machine, a small amount of input, one output. It should be memorable.

### Method Layers

Good for: methodology frameworks, system layers, capability stacks, content-system layers.

How to draw it: layers of boxes, but not a formal pyramid. Xiaohei builds or carries pieces near the structure.

### Map Route

Good for: idea-to-launch, user path, content handoff path, learning route.

How to draw it: one curved path with a few nodes. Xiaohei pulls a line or walks the path.

### Mini Comic Sequence

Good for: failure to success, real process, critique, before/after use.

How to draw it: 2-4 small scenes, each expressing one action.

## Original Metaphor Method

Invent a fresh metaphor from the current article every time. Do not copy old images.

### Three Steps

1. Turn the abstract concept into a physical action: getting stuck, leaking, becoming heavy, sorting, settling, fermenting, opening a door, folding, unpacking, or flowing back.
2. Turn the system structure into a low-tech object: broken machine, cardboard box, drawer, pipe, mailbox, strange dial, scale, well, ladder, or odd workstation.
3. Let Xiaohei perform the action: not standing nearby, but getting stuck in the machine, pulling the wrong wire, guarding a gate, carrying, repairing, weighing, holding a ladder, recording, or stuffing things into a strange device.

### Object Pool

- Cardboard box, drawer, old machine, funnel, scale, mailbox, door, well, ladder, pipe, ball of string, gate, turntable, black box, hole punch, noodle press, clothesline, strange workstation.
- Use only 1-2 objects. Do not fill the canvas.

### Xiaohei Action Pool

- Pull, carry, stuff, fish out, press, weigh, sew, cut, twist, guard, push, catch, dismantle, mark, recycle.
- The action must serve the core meaning. Do not be strange just for strangeness.

## Anti-Copying Rules

Do not open or copy `assets/examples/` by default. These images are only for style calibration: line density, whitespace, restrained color, and Xiaohei's attitude.

Unless the user explicitly says "match this", "copy this composition", or "adapt this example", do not directly reuse these old compositions:

- conveyor belt with two breakpoints
- Xiaohei pulling a judgment lever inside a content machine
- Xiaohei becoming a funnel for sorting traffic, trust, or conversion
- Xiaohei cutting a material fish
- Xiaohei pulling a handoff path
- Xiaohei pulling three layers of information sources
- three Xiaohei figures separately holding a megaphone, building a bridge, and opening a door
- Xiaohei stamping a handoff-copy toolbox
- Xiaohei holding a sign while looking at a common-pitfalls path

For similar themes, switch to a new metaphor. A "handoff path" does not have to be a route; Xiaohei could attach the tail of a content piece to a doorknob. "One source, many uses" does not have to be a fish; Xiaohei could press one paper ball into several shapes.
